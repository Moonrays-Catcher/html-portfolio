Nick's Picks: Cinema Edition

My top 3 movies you should watch.

1. Count of Monte Cristo

This movie has intrigue, revenge, and moral choices. It is best enjoyed by envisioning the film as having a different storyline than the book.

2. The Sting

Robert Redford and James Gandolfini are fantastic. One of the classic con movies of our time.

3. The Dark Knight

Definitely dark and gritty. It's not for the faint of heart but Heath Ledger is phenomenal as the Joker.
